About Us

Don Safaris Kenya

Eco-friendly Safari Company – Nairobi, Kenya

Don Safaris Kenya is a reputable and eco-friendly safari company owned and based in Nairobi, offering safaris across East Africa. Our team is dedicated to giving you a lifetime memorable safari. Sit back and let us handle your holiday from start to finish.

We provide a 24-hour support system to answer your queries. Our multilingual, highly trained driver guides ensure that every drive between destinations and every game drive is enjoyable, engaging, and full of information about our heritage.

Our Vision

  • Become a leader in luxury safaris in East Africa
  • Become an ambassador of sustainable tourism
  • Ensure customer experience and satisfaction is our daily goal
  • Support vulnerable communities through CSR
